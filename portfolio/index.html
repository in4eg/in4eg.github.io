Why being a designer with frontend skills is an advantage

With frontend experience, I approach design not only from an aesthetic point of view, but with a deep understanding of technical implementation.
  • Design realistic and feasible interfaces that adapt easily to different devices
  • Collaborate effectively with developers, because I speak the same language
  • Optimize the process by anticipating technical limitations or opportunities at the mockup stage
  • Create interactive prototypes that not only look good, but work like a real product
  • Ensure better consistency between design and development, improving the user experience
