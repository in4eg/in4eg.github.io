Ukropter operates at the intersection of volunteering, engineering, and military logistics, with two main activities: a charitable foundation and the development & production of modern drones for the Armed Forces of Ukraine.
Target audiences:
  • Military units of the Armed Forces needing drones
  • Volunteers, donors, philanthropists
  • International investors and business partners
  • Journalists, media, thought leaders
The Ukropter Foundation is organized jointly with Creative Practice School with support from League Design Agency.
Tasks within the contest:
  • Main page
  • Landing page “Support the Army”
  • Propose the website structure

  • Visual design development (UI)
