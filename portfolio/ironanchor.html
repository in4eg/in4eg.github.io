Iron Anchor Tattoo Studio is a professional tattoo studio with extensive experience and four main artistic directions. The website was designed to fully showcase the studio’s activity, introduce the artists, ease clients’ fears before getting a tattoo, and provide a simple way to book a session.

Key points:

  • A tattoo studio with a strong portfolio and four core styles, presented through a visually expressive and structured digital experience.
  • The website focuses on transparency, trust, and education helping users overcome hesitation, understand the process, and feel confident before booking.
  • The core visual metaphor is ink in water combined with strong straight lines, symbolizing both fluid creativity and precision.

Website goals:

  • Showcase the studio’s work, atmosphere, and expertise in a compelling and authentic way.
  • Introduce tattoo artists, their styles, and portfolios to help clients choose the right master.
  • Reduce fear and uncertainty through clear explanations of the tattoo process and aftercare.
  • Create a smooth and intuitive booking flow for scheduling a tattoo session.
  • Build credibility and trust through structured content and strong visual identity.

UX Research:

  • UX Criteria definition
  • Competitor UX analysis
  • Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)
  • Problem / Solution synthesis
  • Information Architecture & Sitemap
  • User Personas

Project scope:

  • Design of 9 fully developed website pages
  • Mobile version adaptation

UI Design:

  • Moodboard development inspired by ink flowing in water
  • Color system reflecting deep black tones, contrast, and subtle gradients
  • Typography selection combining expressive display fonts with clean straight-line structures
  • Visual metaphor implementation: fluid ink forms balanced with strict geometric lines
