Ukrainian game development company best known for its work in the real-time tactical strategy genre.

Key Points:

  • Specialization: Realistic tactical military strategy games with deep unit control and advanced battlefield simulation
  • Signature engine: Development of the GEM engine, optimized for destructible environments, line-of-sight systems and detailed infantry/vehicle mechanics

Website Goals:

  • Show potential publishers and investors who the company is and what it does
  • Show engine features and games to licensees
  • Help players quickly find resources and support for specific games
  • Demonstrate that the company operates actively and transparently

Project Stages:

  • Competitor analysis (UX Research)
  • Interaction scenario analysis for each persona (UX Research)
  • Website structure development (Site map)
  • Creation of detailed schematic layouts (Hi-Fi Wireframes)
  • Design concept development (UI)
  • Moodboard creation
  • Development of UI graphic elements (UI Kit)
  • Visual design: 13 pages (UI)
  • Presentation preparation
  • Case study preparation and documentation
