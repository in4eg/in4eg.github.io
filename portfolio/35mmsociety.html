35mm Society is a brand for those who value grain, light, imperfection, and spontaneity. A store dedicated to film aesthetics and film cameras with accessories.

Key points:

  • A store built around film aesthetics that celebrates real moments, grain, light, and the magic of analog photography. Products include disposable cameras, film boxes, memory-shooting kits, and accessories.
  • The brand is not about technical perfection but emotionality and warmth. Its unique feature is the "film letter" as a special way of communicating with users.

Website goals:

  • Create an online store for selling cameras, film, kits, and accessories.
  • Showcase the brand's atmosphere through a retro style inspired by digital film photography.
  • Ensure a simple and fast purchase flow with filters, categories, and convenient payment.
  • Build trust through brand storytelling, a blog, and communication with customers.
  • Highlight the uniqueness and cultural value of analog photography.

Project workflow:

  • Competitor analysis within the analog photography niche (UX Research)
  • User scenario analysis for personas: creative youth, photographers, collectors, beginners (UX Research)
  • Creation of a full site map
  • Lo-Fi wireframes
  • Hi-Fi wireframes
  • Moodboard development with retro posters and film-inspired aesthetics
  • UI Kit creation: monospace typography, pastel colors, “grain” and “slow transition” effects
  • Development of graphic interface elements (UI Kit)
  • Design of key pages: home, catalog, product page, checkout
  • Documentation and structuring of the design process (Case Study)
