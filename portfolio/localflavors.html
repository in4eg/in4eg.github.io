LocalFlavors is more than just visiting cafes. It's a delicious journey through your city streets where you discover locals' favorite spots, try unexpected flavor combinations, and learn the stories behind cuisines from various cultures. What does the company offer?
  • Tastings of unique local dishes and drinks
  • Stories about the gastronomy of your neighborhood
  • The possibility to order a personalized route

Landing Page Goals:

  • Register for the next food tour in your city
  • Attract attention to the company
  • Encourage trust and emotional engagement

  • Hi-Fi wireframe development

  • Visual design: full + mobile versions (UI)
