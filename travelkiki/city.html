Information about London

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is one of the top world destinations, whether it’s holidays, short trips, shopping, studies or work you are looking for. As a result, it comes as no surprise that in London you will probably meet people from all over the world who live, work and coexist harmoniously.

The multicultural spirit is strongly reflected among the 7.7 million residents of London. Locals and foreigners – students or not – really create a perfect mosaic of cultures and souls, having one enormous attribute in common: the city itself!
