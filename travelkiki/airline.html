Flights from Greece to London

London, the city of Big Ben, Buckingham Palace and the famous red double-decker buses! If you are looking for cheap airtickets to London, then you are about to visit the second most important city in the world, following New York.

A cosmopolitan metropolis, financial centre and a top notch art, fashion and culture hub, London is so vast – and yet so well structured. All the different cultures,

colours, scents and auras of the world are concentrated at the melting pot of the neighbourhoods of London, each and every one of them with its own unique character.

Once you book your accommodation in one of the cheap hotels you’ll find in London, you will be free to go out and explore. There is so much to see, taste, listen to and experience!

Check in for Lufthansa flights.
You can register for Lufthansa flights via the Internet, using a mobile application or at the airport before departure.
Online check-in opens 23 hours and closes 40 minutes before departure. Boarding pass must be printed, it can be done independently, at the airline counter or in a special machine. The time of beginning and end of check-in for Lufthansa flights at the airport depends on the specific flight. The exact time can be found when purchasing tickets.
