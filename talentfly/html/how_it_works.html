How it Works

TalentFly connects employers in specific industries with candidates who are seeking new career options. We pair both parties based on their distinct requirements, and in doing so, we facilitate a perfect fit both for the employer and the candidate.

Our user-friendly and unscripted interviews are led by industry experts and prompt candidates to answer the questions that hiring managers need answered.

This affords candidates an exclusive opportunity to showcase their talents and connect with leading employers with which they may not have otherwise had the chance to interview.

Plus, with tools our innovative tools like keyword searches, hiring managers can view digital interviews with ease and efficiency to determine whether candidates are viable fits to fill their open positions.

How Our Process Works
  • Account Setup

    The candidate sets up a TalentFly account and inputs information regarding their employment history, hard and soft skills, awards, education, and so forth.

  • Interview Invitation

    The candidate receives details about when their interview will take place. They prepare for the interview by answering TalentFly’s practice questions, refreshing themselves on their work history and standout achievements, and getting ready as they would for any other interview, with the knowledge that this important video may be viewed by dozens of hiring managers at competitive employers in their target industry.

  • Unscripted Interview

    The unscripted interview takes place and is led by an expert in the candidate’s industry. The candidate will be prompted to answer both basic and challenging questions that showcase their skills and demonstrate why they’d be an asset to a potential employer’s team.

  • Sharing Interview

    After the interview is complete, the candidate can share it among their network as a tool that highlights their experience and capabilities. Hiring managers, recruiters, and other professionals seeking new hires will also be able to view it.

  • Hand-selected Candidates Profiles

    For employers who wish to fast-track the hiring process, TalentFly can provide links to a number of candidates who have been hand-selected to fill their open positions based on criteria provided.

  • Watch & Browse Video Interviews

    Hiring managers can view the digital interviews performed by the candidates that have been chosen for them and skip directly to specific points of interest using the keyword search function.

  • Exchange Contacts

    Once a hiring manager determines that a particular employee is an ideal fit for their open position, they can exchange contact information and begin their own screening process, if desired. The employer can repeat the process for any additional positions they may have open in the company.
